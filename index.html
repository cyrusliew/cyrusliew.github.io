    leather apron

    NOV 2014 | Technician, Programming

    Website

    "Why are they so cold?" is often a word we asked to a depression victim. Depression isn’t something where you can tell them to get over it, but it’s a long going process and patience that is needed to be there for them. Although there isn’t really the right cure for them, but what we could at least do is to talk and listen to their untold story.

    This is an installation project that uses sound to interact with the mechanism and unfold the truth of the interactive storytelling.
